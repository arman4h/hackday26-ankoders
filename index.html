Welcome to Silent Bridge

Instant Communicate between teacher and simple becomes more comfortable. Select your role below to access your personalized dashboard.

How It Works

1

For Students

Enter your name and select from priority-based request buttons to communicate with your teacher non-verbally. Each request is color-coded by urgency level.

2

Request Types

Status Messages: "I understand", "I didn't understand", "Task Completed" - Teacher marks as "Seen"

Urgent Requests: "Urgent", "Feeling Sick" - Teacher marks as "Seen"

Action Requests: "Request to Step Out" - Teacher can Accept or Reject

3

For Teachers

View all student requests in real-time, sorted by priority. Urgent requests (red) appear first. Respond with appropriate actions based on request type.

4

Priority System

Requests are automatically prioritized and color-coded: Urgent High Medium Low
